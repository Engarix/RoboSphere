# Controlller

## Controls:
### Basic Movement
* UP - Forward
* DOWN - Backwards
* LEFT - Standing still, left wheel forward, right wheel backwards
* RIGHT - Standing still, left wheel backwards, right wheel forwards

### Advanced Movement
* UP + LEFT - Forward with left turn 45 degrees
* UP + RIGHT - Forward with right turn 45 degrees
* DOWN + LEFT - Backwards with left turn 45 degrees
* DOWN + RIGHT - Backwards with right turn 45 degrees

## Commands:
### MOVE
MOVE
